Active Programs

NMSA Initiatives

Programmes and projects advancing maritime safety, training, infrastructure, and community awareness across Papua New Guinea.

Status: Active

Ongoing Projects

Maritime & Waterways Safety

Improving navigational aids, marker buoys, and community safety awareness across 6 maritime provinces to reduce vessel accidents and fatalities in PNG waters.

Ongoing

Small Craft Safety Act Rollout

Implementing the Small Craft Act across all provinces — registering dinghies and outboards, training operators, and deploying safety equipment to remote coastal villages.

In Progress

Engine Room Simulator

Procuring and installing a state-of-the-art engine room simulator at the PNG Maritime College to modernize training for the next generation of marine engineers.

Development

Aids to Navigation Upgrade

Replacing aging lighthouses and buoys with solar-powered, self-monitoring navigational aids across key shipping lanes, ports, and reef passages throughout PNG.

Ongoing

National Oil Spill Contingency Plan

Reviewing and updating PNG's National Oil Spill Contingency Plan, pre-positioning response equipment, and training regional response teams across 5 coastal locations.

In Progress

STCW Training Enhancement

Upgrading approved maritime training institutions with new curricula, simulators, and assessment systems to meet current STCW 2010 Manila Amendments requirements.

Active
Completed

Past Achievements

Port State Control Audit

Successfully completed a comprehensive audit of all international vessels calling at PNG's 4 major commercial ports, achieving a 95% deficiency correction rate.

Completed 2024

GMDSS Station Upgrade

Upgraded the MRCC Port Moresby's Global Maritime Distress and Safety System (GMDSS) communications infrastructure to IMO standards.

Completed 2023

Coastal Community Awareness

Reached over 12,000 coastal community members across 8 provinces with water safety, lifejacket usage, and emergency communication training campaigns.

Completed 2023
