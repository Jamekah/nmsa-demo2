Core Functions

What We Protect

01

Search & Rescue

Coordinating rapid emergency response operations across PNG waters via the Maritime Rescue Coordination Centre (MRCC), operating around the clock to save lives at sea.

02

Vessel Safety

Enforcing rigorous inspections, surveys, and registration standards for all vessels operating in PNG territorial waters and under the PNG flag internationally.

03

Marine Environment

Protecting our oceans and coastlines through pollution prevention, oil spill response readiness, and enforcement of environmental regulations across all ports.

Active Programs

Current Initiatives

Maritime & Waterways Safety

Improving navigational aids, marker buoys, and community safety awareness across 6 maritime provinces to reduce vessel accidents and fatalities.

Ongoing

Small Craft Safety Act

Rolling out the Small Craft Act framework to register dinghies and small vessels, train operators, and improve safety standards in coastal communities.

In Progress

Engine Room Simulator

Modernizing maritime training infrastructure with state-of-the-art simulation technology for the next generation of PNG marine engineers and officers.

Our Authority

Regulating PNG's Seas with Precision & Purpose

Established under the National Maritime Safety Authority Act, NMSA is the statutory body responsible for maritime safety, marine environment protection, and seafarer certification across Papua New Guinea.

We work in close partnership with international maritime organizations, regional governments, and local communities to uphold the highest standards of safety at sea.
